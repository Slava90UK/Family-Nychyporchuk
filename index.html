Here is the chronology of our family's life:

  • 2018 - Our acquaintance began with a Facebook conversation.

  • 2019 - We got married. It was a wonderful honeymoon year.

  • 2020 - We both lost our jobs this year and had a great time at home for about six months. It was the year of the coronavirus.

  • 2021 - This year, our rose-colored glasses fell off, and we began to see each other's flaws.

  • 2022 - This year was a year of loss, upheaval and the outbreak of war in Ukraine. But our marriage has only gotten stronger.

  • 2023 - We have adjusted several areas of our lives. Our personal relationship was like the waves of the sea.

  • 2024 - This year, God has given us a son. We have changed our place of residence several times.

  • 2025 - We've been through a lot together. We are very glad that we have each other. We love each other and wish you the same.

We have a deep understanding that each of us can make this world a little better if we start with ourselves. This principle is the key to success in any relationship and in any business. We always try to appreciate every person who comes our way. And if you're reading this, we wish God would bless you! With respect from Slava and Alyona!
